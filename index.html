About Me

Less is more.

My name is Goran Jugovic, and I am a graphic designer based in Bosnia. Clients often consider me an out-of-the-box creative artist. My minimalist style is a reflection of my belief that less is more.

When it comes to LOGO design, my approach is to focus on creating distinct visuals that tell a story through their structure. I use shapes and lines to make each logo unique yet recognizable, and I always make sure to include subtleties that reflect my client's values or mission statement. By doing so, I add another layer to the brand identity as a whole, making it more impactful and memorable.

Whether you are a startup seeking a logo that will help you stand out, or an established company in need of a logo refresh, I am here to assist you! WORLDWIDE
